BEST 10 Actor/Actress

Cillian Murphy



Cillian Murphy is an Irish actor born in 1976, best known for his magnetic portrayal of Thomas Shelby in Peaky Blinders (2013–2022). With a career built on intensity and versatility, he won the Academy Award for Best Actor for Oppenheimer (2023) and is widely considered one of the finest actors of his generation.

Anthony Hopkins



Sir Anthony Hopkins is a Welsh actor born in 1937, best known for his iconic portrayal of Hannibal Lecter in The Silence of the Lambs (1991). With over six decades of career, he has won two Academy Awards and remains one of the most commanding and respected actors in cinema history.

Brendan Fraser

Brendan Fraser is an American-Canadian actor born in 1968, best known for his adventurous role as Rick O'Connell in The Mummy (1999). After years away from the spotlight, he made a stunning comeback winning the Academy Award for Best Actor for his deeply emotional performance in The Whale (2022), one of Hollywood's most celebrated comebacks in recent memory.

Will Smith

Will Smith is an American actor and rapper born in 1968, best known for his charismatic performances in blockbusters like Men in Black, Ali, and The Pursuit of Happyness. He won the Academy Award for Best Actor for King Richard (2021), though his legacy was momentarily overshadowed by the infamous Oscars slapping incident in 2022. Despite the controversy, he remains one of Hollywood's most recognizable and influential stars.

Emma Stone

Emma Stone is an American actress born in 1988, best known for her charming and versatile performances in films like La La Land (2016) and Poor Things (2023). She has won two Academy Awards for Best Actress, cementing her place as one of Hollywood's most talented and sought-after actresses of her generation.

Jessie Buckley

Jessie Buckley is an Irish actress born in 1989, best known for her raw and captivating performances in films like Wild Rose (2018) and The Lost Daughter (2021), the latter earning her an Academy Award nomination. Equally gifted on stage and screen, she is widely regarded as one of the most exciting and fearless talents to emerge in recent years.

Rose Byrne

Rose Byrne is an Australian actress born in 1979, best known for her sharp comedic timing in Bridesmaids (2011) and the Neighbors franchise, as well as her dramatic work in series like Damages. Effortlessly transitioning between comedy and drama, she is regarded as one of the most versatile and underrated actresses working in Hollywood today.
